Five Nights at Freddy's: Reincarnation

Five Nights at Freddy's: Reincarnation is a fan game inspired by Five Nights at Freddy's 1 by Scott Cawthon made entirely in VRChat using Udon and Unity. This project serves as both passion project and first project under Raging Atlas Games Studio


Features include, but are not limited to: a genuine replication of the FNaF1 AI, a functional computer for handling cameras and other game mechanics (see below), support for player-controlled AI, and Raycast-Based sound physics.
