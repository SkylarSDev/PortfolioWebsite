Epsilon 11

Epsilon 11 was a VR psychological horror shooter that focused in on the experience a Mobile Task Force agent would have on the job, highlighting just how brutal and horrible dealing with the eldritch horrors of the SCP Foundation can be. Heavily inspired by games like Ready or Not and SCP: Labrat.
